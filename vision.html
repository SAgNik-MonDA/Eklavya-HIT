Our Vision & Mission

"Empowering through compassion. Building through action."

Our Vision

Team Eklavya envisions a world where every animal receives compassionate care and every child has access to quality education. We strive to create communities where kindness toward all living beings is the foundation of a more equitable society.

Through our dedicated efforts in animal welfare and child education, we aim to foster empathy, resilience, and hope for generations to come. We believe in creating lasting change by starting small, helping people with their urgent needs now, while also working to fix the bigger problems over time.

Our Mission

Team Eklavya is dedicated to creating meaningful change through direct action and sustainable programs that address the needs of both animals and children in our communities.

Animal Welfare

Rescue, feed, treat, and provide care for street animals in need, creating a compassionate approach to animal welfare.

Child Education

Provide quality education, resources, and awareness programs for underprivileged children to build a foundation for their future.

Community Service

Organize special events like mass food distribution, hosting awareness programmes on several sensitive issues of the society and celebrating cultural events to get in touch with their roots.

Team Eklavya

A Social Welfare society of Haldia Institute of Technology dedicated to animal welfare and child education, making a difference one life at a time.
