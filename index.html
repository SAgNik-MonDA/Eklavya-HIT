Sulakshmir Sachetan

Organised in 2025


An awareness drive for young girls and women’s health, held every Women’s Day with love, care, and purpose. Held every Women's Day, this event focuses on teaching young girls about good touch and bad touch, and spreading menstrual hygiene awareness among underprivileged women. We also distribute sanitary pads and guide them on safe practices, aiming to build confidence, awareness, and dignity in every woman and girl we reach.

Sterilisation Program

Organised in 2024


Eklavya’s Sterilization Program promotes humane animal population control through safe and ethical procedures. Partnering with local veterinarians, we organize camps to sterilize stray animals, reducing suffering and preventing overpopulation. Each surgery includes vaccinations and post-op care, ensuring animal welfare while creating healthier communities. Join us in building a sustainable future for street animals.

Students' Survey 2k25

Organised in 2025


Eklavya, the Social Welfare Society of Haldia Institute of Technology, successfully conducted Student Survey 2k25, a grassroots initiative to assess the quality of education in Haldia. The findings aim to highlight challenges and advocate for better educational opportunities.
